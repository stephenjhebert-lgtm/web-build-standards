# Reps & Reels — Homepage Copy (v1 draft)
**Stream A1 deliverable** — 2026-04-19
**Voice reference:** `/Reps and Reels/Brand/brand-voice-guide.md`
**Status:** Draft 1. Every line below has been passed against the brand voice guide's never-use list and the five-word DNA (Humble, Dry, Self-aware, Disciplined, Understated).

---

## Voice guardrails used while writing

- No motivational clichés (grind, hustle, beast mode, level up, no days off)
- No hype language (crushing it, absolutely obsessed, game-changer)
- No AI openers ("In today's fast-paced world", "It's not just about X, it's about Y")
- No brand announcing ("I'm going to start sharing more of...")
- Contractions preferred. "Ya" not "Yeah" where the register allows.
- Specificity beats abstraction — real time markers, real tune names where they land.
- Reads like Steve wrote it at 9pm, not a brand manager at 2pm.

## 1. Hero (LOCKED — Option C)

> **H1:** Two hobbies nobody asked me to combine.
>
> **Subhead:** Competitive lifting. Competitive piping. Same person. The overlap is the whole thing.

**Why locked:** Fan-first audience hierarchy wins. The self-aware opener matches how Steve actually posts ("accidental denim," "no idea what it adds"), and the subhead earns its finish with the parallel structure the voice guide protects. Brand partnership managers who squint were never the design driver.

**Design note for A2:** H1 needs to land in under 5 seconds with zero scroll. That means the type does most of the work. Heavy display weight, tight leading, no decoration competing with it. Subhead should sit at about 35-40% the size of the H1 and feel like the quiet follow-up.

> Not a fitness page. Not a pipe band page. The overlap — gym bag and pipe case in the same car, same person carrying them, neither activity explaining the other. Most of what I post is that. Errors left in. Uploads happen when they happen.

**Notes:**

## 4. About / origin (short, personal, not earnest)

> Twenty-plus years of lifting. Five years on the chanter — fifteen on the drum before that. CRM engagement manager by day, which is where most of the actual editing happens. Started Reps & Reels because the two things sit next to each other in my life and I wanted to post about them without explaining the combination every time.
>
> I'm not an expert. Just consistent. Most weeks.

**Notes:**
- Specific time markers (20+, 5, 15) are on-brand.
- "CRM engagement manager by day" — one credential drop, no expansion. If a recruiter lands here, they get the signal without the site becoming a resume.
- "Most weeks" is the self-aware disclaimer that keeps the "consistent" claim honest. Pulled almost verbatim from the TikTok bio the voice guide protects.
- Kept under 80 words. Brief says paragraph length, not page length. This is ~70 words.
