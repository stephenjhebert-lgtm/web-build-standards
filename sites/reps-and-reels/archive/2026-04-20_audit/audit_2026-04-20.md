# Reps & Reels — Site Audit & Rebuild Notes
**Date:** 2026-04-20
**Audited file:** `homepage_prototype.html` (rewritten in place)

---

## What was wrong (the audit)

| # | Issue | Why it killed engagement |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Hero was a void.** First 60vh was effectively black. | Nothing earned the scroll. No reason to keep going. |
| 2 | **Brand mark was muddied to 25% opacity** behind H1, causing the H1 to land directly on top of the crest's own "REPS & REELS / QUIET WORK WINS" text. | Two competing typographic layers = visual noise that reads as a draft. |
| 3 | **Top-left logo at 28px.** | Effectively invisible. The brand never "claimed" the page. |
| 4 | **Palette was 99% black.** No brass moment above the fold. | Brand promise (iron + flowers / 75/25 ratio) was invisible. Felt like a generic dark-mode SaaS hero. |
| 5 | **Headline was clean but timid.** "Two hobbies nobody asked me to combine." is a tagline, not a hook — it doesn't immediately tell you *which two hobbies*. | The juxtaposition (lifting + bagpipes) is the whole hook. Burying it = burying the reason to scroll. |
| 6 | **No movement, no rhythm.** | Page felt static. Nothing pulled the eye downward. |
| 7 | **Sections didn't have visual identity.** Just generic top-borders separated dark sections. | No "chapter" feeling. Whole page read as one undifferentiated dark slab. |

---

## What changed (the rebuild)

### Hero
- **Brand crest is now the foreground subject** at full opacity, with a brass radial glow behind it. Mobile: stacked above the headline. Desktop: right column of a 2-col grid.
- **Brass radial gradient + warm-black base** replaces the flat #0B0B0C void. Adds atmosphere without going decorative.
- **Headline rewrite:** "Iron in one hand. Pipes in the other." — names the two things in the first 4 seconds. "Pipes" is set in brass for a one-color accent moment.
- **Brass rule + meta line** ("@repsreels • INSTAGRAM · TIKTOK") confirms platform identity above the fold.
- **Scroll cue:** mono "KEEP SCROLLING — THE OVERLAP" with a pulsing brass line. Earns the "why scroll" answer.

### Identity & nav
- **Logo upgraded from 28px → 44/52px** (mobile/desktop) with drop shadow.
- **Wordmark restructured** into stacked "REPS & REELS" (display, 18–22px) + "QUIET WORK WINS." tag (mono brass, 9–10px). Reads as a real masthead, not an icon caption.

### Movement
- **Brass marquee strip** under the hero (TRAIN · MUSIC · DISCIPLINE · QUIET WORK WINS · ERRORS LEFT IN · YEAR X ON THIS TUNE), 38s loop, brand-vocabulary phrases. Movement that does brand work.

### Section rhythm — alternating dark / bone-paper
- **01 The Premise** → bone paper, oxblood accent, brass highlight on key phrase, cornerstone in display type
- **02 The Feed** → forge black, reels grid with FPO images, brass platform pills
- **03 Origin** → bone paper, oxblood, NEW: stat block (20+ / 5 / 3) for instant credibility
- **04 Contact** → forge black, brass-outlined form card

### Texture
- SVG-noise grain overlay on every dark section (8% opacity, overlay blend) to break the digital-clean feel and lean into the gritty/analog brand.

### Typography hierarchy
- H1 scaled up: 54 → 144px (was 48 → 112). Mobile cover-magazine effect intensified.
- Display weight 900 used for cornerstone quote, marquee, stats, footer wordmark — same DNA throughout.
- Brass `<em>` accent inside H1, H2, contact heading — single-color emphasis system.

## Specs for any new images you want to commission

You said you're okay with missing elements. Below are the **highest-leverage** asset commissions if/when you want to fill them.

### A. Reel thumbnails (current `/fpo/reel_01.jpg` → `reel_06.jpg`)
**Status:** placeholder numbered images now. Final: pull stills from real IG/TikTok posts.
**Specs:**
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (matches reel embed slot)
- **Min size:** 720 × 1280px (1080 × 1920 ideal)
- **Format:** JPG (or AVIF/WebP)
- **Treatment:** subtle grain + slight desaturation (-10–15%); brass-tinted highlights OK; **no full faces** in thumbnails — hands, chanter, plates, silhouettes
- **File size target:** <120KB each (lazy-loaded)

### B. Hero crest — SVG version (currently using `brand_mark_primary_gold.png`)
**Status:** raster looks great but soft on retina above ~520px wide.
**Specs to give your designer:**
- **Format:** SVG, optimized (≤30KB)
- **Dimensions:** square, 1024 × 1024 viewBox
- **Style:** trace existing `brand_mark_primary_gold.png` with cleaner vector edges; preserve grain only as a subtle noise overlay (or omit and rely on CSS grain layer)
- **Color:** keep current aged-brass `#C9A24B` as fill; no shadow baked in (the page adds drop-shadow)
- **Why:** crisp at any scale, lighter weight, no LCP penalty
